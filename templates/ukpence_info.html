⚖️ The Wealth Tax
A progressive tax on passive earnings once your balance reaches 10,000 UKP. Works like income tax brackets — each slice of an earning is taxed at the rate of the bracket it lands in. Tax goes back to the bank.
Balance BracketTax Rate
Under 10,000 UKP0%
10,000 – 20,000 UKP25%
20,000 – 30,000 UKP50%
30,000 – 50,000 UKP75%
Over 50,000 UKP90%
Exempt earnings: prediction wins, wager wins, refunds, /pay transfers, and the welcome bonus are never taxed. The wealth tax only hits passive bank-funded rewards.
⏳ Inactivity Tax
Every Friday, users who haven't chatted in 60+ days lose 5% of their balance back to the bank. Stay active to keep what you've earned.
