UKPence Guide

The official currency of HMS Victory — a closed economy where every UKP is accounted for.

Filed · Treasury Bench Issued to all crew Index № UKP–001
Total Reserves
800,000UKP
Total Economy · Fixed Forever

UKPence is split between every user's wallet and the Server Bank. When you earn from passive activity, the bank pays you. When you spend on the shop or place a bet, your UKP goes back to the bank. No new UKP is ever created — the total always equals 800,000.

§ 01 / VII

How to Earn

Income · Bank-funded & peer
i.
Chatting Scales with wealth

Chance of +1 UKP every 10 minutes of activity. The richer you are, the rarer the payout — drops to 0 once you hit 10,000 UKP.

ii.
Top Chatter Daily Taxed if rich

+50 UKP awarded daily to the 5 most active chatters of the previous day.

iii.
Stage Participation Taxed if rich

+1 UKP per full minute you spend in a Stage channel.

iv.
Server Booster Daily Taxed if rich

+10 UKP per day for every member who boosts the server.

v.
Prediction Wins Tax-free

Bet on outcomes via the prediction system. Winners split the pot — no tax applied, even at high balances.

vi.
Wager Wins Tax-free

Challenge another user. Winner takes the full pot. Skill-based earnings are not taxed.

vii.
Welcome Bonus

+10 UKP the first time you participate in the economy.

§ 03

The Wealth Tax

Progressive · On passive earnings

A progressive tax on passive earnings once your balance reaches 10,000 UKP. Works like income tax brackets — each slice of an earning is taxed at the rate of the bracket it lands in. Tax goes back to the bank.

Note · 3a
Exempt earnings: prediction wins, wager wins, refunds, /pay transfers, and the welcome bonus are never taxed. The wealth tax only hits passive bank-funded rewards.
