# HMS-Victory Badges Guide

This guide explains how to earn each of the badges displayed on your rank card. Badges are special achievements awarded for your activity and interactions within the server.

## Current Badges

### 🏆 Hall of Fame
**ID**: `hof`
- **How to earn**: Create a message that is high-quality or funny enough to get forwarded to the #Hall-of-Fame thread. This is typically triggered by reaching a specific reaction threshold (e.g., Starboard-style).

### 🛍️ First Purchase
**ID**: `first_purchase`
- **How to earn**: Use the `/shop` command and successfully purchase your very first item.

### 🤐 Shutcoin User
**ID**: `shutcoin_user`
- **How to earn**: Take part in the server's economy by using a "Shutcoin". This is awarded the first time you use the `:Shut:` reaction to time out another user.

### ⛓️ Chain Linker
**ID**: `reply_chain`
- **How to earn**: Be active in conversations! You earn this by participating in a reply chain—specifically, by sending 3 or more consecutive replies to other users' messages.

### ⚡ Active Chatter
**ID**: `active_chatter`
- **How to earn**: Stay consistent with your activity. This is awarded when you achieve a high level of message activity within a single 24-hour period.

### 🥇 Elite Talker
**ID**: `top_chatter`
- **How to earn**: Be one of the top 5 most active chatters in the server for the day. This is calculated and awarded during the daily summary.

### 🎭 Stage Fan
**ID**: `stage_fan`
- **How to earn**: Attend and participate in Stage Events. Spend a significant amount of time listening or speaking in a Stage channel to unlock this badge.

*This guide will be updated as more badges are added to the system.*
