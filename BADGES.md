# HMS-Victory Badges Guide

### 💰 High Roller
**Rarity**: Gold
Awarded for reaching a balance of 100,000 UKPence.

### 💸 Philanthropist
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for giving away a total of 10,000 UKPence using the `/pay` command.

### 📉 Bankrupt
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for reaching exactly 0 UKPence after having at least 1,000 UKPence previously.

### 🛒 Shopaholic
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for purchasing 10 items from the bot's shop.

### 🎉 Party Animal
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for attending 5 different Stage events.

### 👻 Night Owl
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for sending 100 messages between 2 AM and 5 AM UK time.

### 🔒 The Warden
**Rarity**: Gold
Awarded for successfully using a Shutcoin on 10 different people.

### 🏆 Hall of Fame
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for getting a message into the Hall of Fame.

### 🛍️ First Purchase
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for purchasing your first item from the shop.

### 🤐 Shutcoin User
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for using a Shutcoin for the first time.

### ⛓️ Chain Linker
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for participating in a reply chain of 3 or more messages.

### ⚡ Active Chatter
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for sending 200 or more messages in a single day.

### 🥇 Elite Talker
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for being one of the top 5 daily chatters.

### 🎭 Stage Fan
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for spending significant time in a Stage channel.

### 🎅 Christmas
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for messaging on Christmas day.

### 🎃 Halloween
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for messaging on Halloween.

### 🎙️ Chatterbox
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for spending one hour in a VC session.

### 🖥️ Sharing is Caring
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for screensharing for 30 minutes.

### 🇺🇸 English (Simplified)
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for being caught by the Americanism filter.

### 📣 Fast Hands
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for reacting to an announcement within 10 minutes.

### 📢 Small Talker
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for reacting to a minor announcement within 10 minutes.

### 🔥 Chef
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for using the roast command.

### 💀 Fried
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for being targeted by a roast command.

### 💬 Popular
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for having three people reply to one of your messages.

### 🔇 Silenced
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for being shut by a shutcoin.

### 💎 Supporter
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for boosting the server.

### 👑 Diamond Hands
**Rarity**: Gold
Awarded for boosting the server for a year.

### 🔮 Oracle
**Rarity**: Gold
Awarded for winning 5 predictions in a row.

### 🌧️ Unlucky
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for losing 5 predictions in a row.

### 🎰 High Stakes
**Rarity**: Silver
Awarded for placing a bet of over 5,000 UKPence on a single prediction.

### 🌅 Morning Person
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for sending 50 messages between 6 AM and 9 AM UK time.

### 🎯 Target Practice
**Rarity**: Bronze
Awarded for being the target of the `/roast` command 10 or more times.

### 🎆 New Year, New Me
**Rarity**: Gold

### 💖 Valentine
